Chili

Description

Chili is an easy to make, all-in-one pot, dish that can be easy to make for bigger groups. It pairs well with salads and bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium yellow onion-diced.
  • 1 lb 90% lean ground beef.
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons chili powder.
  • 2 tablespoons ground cumin.
  • 2 tablespoons granulated suger.
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste.
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder.
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt.
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper.
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper.
  • 1 1/2 cups beeef broth.
  • 15 oz. can petite diced tomatos.
  • 16 oz. can red kidney beans, drained and rinsed.
  • 8 oz. tomato sauce.

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large soup pot over medium-high heat for 2 minutes.
  2. Add chopped onion and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add ground beef and break it apart with a wooden cooking spoon.
  4. Cook for 6-7 minutes.
  5. Add chili powder, cumin, sugar, tomato paste, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and cayenne.
  6. Stir until well combined.
  7. Add diced tomatoes, kidney beans, beef broth, and tomato sauce.
  8. Stir well.
  9. Simmmer for 20-25 minutes.
  10. Turn off heat and let rest for 5-10 minutes before serving.
