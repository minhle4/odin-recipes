Baked Ziti Recipe

Description

This Italian-American dish is a staple comfort food made to fill those cheesy desires. With the perfect blend of sausage, tomato suace, and cheese everyone around the dinner table with be praising you about your cooking skills.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ziti pasta.
  • 1 tsp of salt.
  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil.
  • 1 lb italian sausage.
  • 1 chopped yellow onion.
  • 4 chopped garlic cloves.
  • 1 tsp minced basil.
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning.
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes.
  • 4 cups tomato sauce.
  • 1 tsp of black pepper.
  • 8 oz of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  • 1 cup grated parmesan.
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until edible.
  3. Drain pot and mix 1 tsp of olive oil in with pasta.
  4. Cook and break up 1 lb of italian sausage over medium-high heat.
  5. Once the sausage is cooked add chopped yellow onion and caramelize.
  6. Add garlic, basil, italian seasoning, red pepper flakes to the pan and stir to mix.
  7. Add tomato sauce then bring to simmer.
  8. Add salt or pepper to taste.
  9. Layer the bottom of a casserole pan with a thin layer of tomato sauce and ricotta cheese.
  10. Pour in meat and pasta into the casserole pan and mix evenly.
  11. Add in th rest of the pasta sauce.
  12. Sprinkle top with mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  13. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes unil the top is slightly browned and cheese melted.
  14. Let sit 10 minutes before serving.
