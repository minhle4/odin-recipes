Turkey Burger Recipe

Description

Turkey burgers are a leaner and healthier alternative to regular beef burgers. They're great on a hot summers day for a family BBQ.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground turkey.
  • 2 minced garlic cloves.
  • 1/2 yellow onion.
  • 1 tsp oregano.
  • 1/2 tsp of salt and pepper(each).
  • 1 whole bun (2 halves).
  • 1 piece roman lettuce

Steps

  1. Mix oregano, salt, pepper, minced garlic, and ground turkey in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Scoop out palm sized ground turkey from bowl and cook on a skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Cook meat to preffered taste
  4. Remove meat from skillet and add onto a bun
  5. Add lettuce, cut tomato slices, and yellow onion slices
